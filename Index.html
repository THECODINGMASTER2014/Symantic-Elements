Facts About Cat

Cats are fascinating creatures with a plethora of interesting facts. They are known for their agility and ability to land on their feet, thanks to their flexible spine and "righting reflex". Cats can jump up to six times their height and can run surprisingly fast. They are also known for their long sleep cycles, typically spending 12-16 hours a day sleeping. Furthermore, cats have unique characteristics like unique nose prints, a lack of the ability to taste sweetness, and the ability to rotate their ears 180 degrees.
